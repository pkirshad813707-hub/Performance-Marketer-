Product Description

The fine print nobody reads.

I've spent the better part of a decade turning "can you just quickly fix this" into actual, functioning design systems. It's less glamorous than it sounds. It's exactly as glamorous as it sounds.

I care about type, whitespace, and shipping things that don't embarrass me six months later — in roughly that order. I've been told I have "strong opinions, loosely held, aggressively defended."

When I'm not designing, I'm probably overthinking a font pairing for something nobody asked me to redesign. It's a lifestyle, not a hobby.
